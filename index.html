About Me

Bio

Born and raised in a very small town in the north of germany, near Vechta. Third kid of four, I spent my childhood on a farmlike proberty. Here I could try out and learn a lot of thing and could grap all the tools I liked. I am very grateful for that. I was always very intersted in technical things. After I finished school with ups and downs, I started to study mechanical engineering. For me this was the most technical job field, and due to my high interest in this field, I could achive a degree dispite the fact this is a hard field. Which I did.

My first job was at Stankowitz Test Equipments over the Engineering Supplier Ferchau Enginueering. They are working on test stands for the automotive industry, especially car seats. Here I first did programming in a professional context,with LabVIEW, which I really liked. After this I switched, also via Ferchau, to the Boge Elastmetall GmbH, where I also used to write my Masters Thesis. Here I had the chance to work in the testing field, and develope and write code for test stands. This time it was often about noice, vibration and harshness (NVH) and durability testing. We had a lot to deal with physics, I gained a lot of practical experience in this field.

I am a very curious person, and I like to learn new things. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. I am also a team player and enjoy working with others to achieve common goals. I believe that communication and collaboration are key to success in any project.
